This fiasco only exists on X11 with the wacom driver. (Source: Qt's TabletEvent)

Commands:
- xinput list
- xinput list-props <dev>
- xinput test-xi2
- xinput test-xi2 <dev>
- udevadm info /dev/input/eventXX
- udevadm info -a /dev/input/eventXX
- (A small custom utility using libwacom)
